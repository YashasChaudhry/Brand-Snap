Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2025  ·  Effective: 16 June 2025

📋 Summary: Brand Snap collects only the information you voluntarily give us (name, phone, business type) to provide our services. We do not sell your data. We do not use tracking cookies. We contact you only via WhatsApp or phone to respond to your enquiry.

1. Who We Are

Brand Snap is a student-led digital agency founded by Yashas Chaudhry, operating at thebrandsnap.in and based in Delhi NCR, India. We build websites and digital presence solutions for local businesses across India.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Phone / WhatsApp: +91 98114 46304
  • Website: thebrandsnap.in

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, phone number, business type, and message.
  • WhatsApp messages — messages you send to our WhatsApp number (+91 98114 46304).
  • Phone calls — your number when you call us directly.
  • Project information — business details, logo files, images, or content you share with us during a project.

We do not collect:

  • Payment card details (we use UPI and bank transfer — processed outside this website).
  • Sensitive personal data (Aadhaar numbers, PAN, financial data, health information).
  • Data from minors under 18 years of age.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or quote request.
  • Deliver the website or digital service you have commissioned.
  • Send you updates about your active project.
  • Follow up after project delivery to offer support.

We do not use your information for advertising, do not sell it to third parties, and do not add you to marketing lists without your explicit consent.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any third-party tracking technology. No data about your browsing behaviour on this site is collected or stored.

The only external resources loaded by this website are Google Fonts (for typography). Google Fonts may collect minimal technical data (such as your IP address) as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We use the CSS API which does not set cookies.

5. WhatsApp Communication

When you click "WhatsApp Us" on this website, you are redirected to WhatsApp (operated by Meta Platforms, Inc.). Your use of WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. Brand Snap does not control WhatsApp's data practices.

Any messages you send us via WhatsApp are stored only within the WhatsApp application and are used solely to respond to your enquiry and deliver your project.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party, except:

  • With your explicit consent — for example, if we need to involve a third-party developer or designer on your project.
  • Legal requirement — if required by Indian law, court order, or government authority.

7. Data Storage & Security

Project-related files and communications are stored on personal devices and WhatsApp belonging to Brand Snap team members. We take reasonable precautions to secure this data, including device-level password protection.

We retain your contact information and project files for up to 2 years after project completion, after which they are deleted. You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 9).

8. Your Rights Under Indian Law

As a resident of India, you have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA). These include:

  • Right to access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — you may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure — you may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to grievance redressal — you may raise a complaint with us or with the Data Protection Board of India.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp at +91 98114 46304. We will respond within 30 days.

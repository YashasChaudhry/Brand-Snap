# 🚀 BrandSnap

BrandSnap is a modern digital agency website built to help local businesses establish a strong online presence through professional websites, Google Business Profile setup, and Instagram business setup.

The platform is designed with a mobile-first, SEO-optimized, and high-performance approach, focused on converting visitors into leads through clean UI/UX and strong call-to-actions.

## 🌐 Services Highlighted
- Website Design & Development  
- Google Business Profile Setup & Optimization  
- Instagram Business Setup  
- Local Business Digital Presence Solutions  

## ⚡ Features
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- SEO optimized structure for Google ranking
- Fast loading and lightweight design
- Lead generation focused layout
- Clean and modern UI/UX
- Conversion-driven call-to-actions (WhatsApp & Call)

## 🎯 Goal
To help small and local businesses get discovered online, build trust, and increase customer inquiries through effective digital presence solutions.

## 📱 Focus
Designed for shops, restaurants, clinics, gyms, salons, and service-based businesses.

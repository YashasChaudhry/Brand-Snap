FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most basic websites are delivered within 48 hours. Business packages take 72 hours. Premium packages are delivered on a priority basis discussed during consultation.

Do I need to provide content and photos?

It helps if you have photos of your business, but we can work with what you have. We'll guide you on what content is needed and help you structure it professionally.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Every website we build is designed mobile-first — it looks and works perfectly on all smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Do you set up hosting and domain too?

We can guide you on purchasing a domain and hosting (often ₹1,500–₹3,000/year), and we'll handle the full technical setup for you.

What is Google Business Profile and why do I need it?

It's your free listing on Google Maps and Search. When customers search for businesses like yours nearby, your profile appears with your address, phone, photos, and reviews — essential for local discovery.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, absolutely. You can start with Basic and upgrade to Business or Premium at any time. We'll apply your existing investment towards the upgrade.

How do I pay?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and other common payment methods. 50% advance is required to begin work, and the remaining 50% on delivery.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

All types of local businesses — restaurants, retail shops, clinics, gyms, salons, coaching centres, beauty parlours, garages, and more. If you have a business, we can build your online presence.

Not sure which plan is right for you?

WhatsApp or call us — we'll understand your business and recommend the best package for free. No pressure, no sales pitch.
